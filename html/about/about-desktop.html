Who are we?

The 115th Coquitlam Scout Group is a passionate youth group that strives on delivering a program that helps develop youth into the leaders of the future. Our program includes many outdoor activities such as hiking, camping, canoeing, rock-climbing and paddleboarding.

Programs

Due to COVID-19, we currently have reduced our programs to just the Scouts section (age 11-14), for the time being.
