Welcome or welcome back to the 115th Coquitlam Scout Section! Whether your child is returning from last year, joining us from another group, or if it’s their first time attending Scouts, we are really excited to have another great year scouting!

This email will contain details on how our group will be implementing Scout Canada’s recommended guidelines. as well as where we will be holding these weekly meetings.


Meeting Location and Time
  • Every Friday from 7:00pm to 8:30pm at 1280 Durant Drive in Coquitlam.
  • We will have a maximum of three meetings each month; there will be no meeting on the fourth or fifth Friday.

Scouts Canada COVID 19 Prevention Measures

As we start a new year of Scouting, Scouts Canada has provided guidelines for groups to follow to prevent the risk of COVID 19 spread.

  • Our area is in Stage 5 of the COVID-19 scouting stages.
  • Stage 5 states that there are no-restrictions on gatherings or sharing of equipment and that camping is allowed for all sections (see here for more details) please see this link for more details:<
  • Our group will require each participant to wear a mask at all times other than when they are eating or drinking.

Outings and Camps

We hope to have many outings and camps this year! Please be sure to check your email for announcements to our outings and camps!

We look forward to seeing all of you this Friday, January 14th at 1280 Durant Drive in Coquitlam. If you have any questions, please send us an email.
