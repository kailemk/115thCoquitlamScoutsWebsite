Welcome to the newly formed 115th Coquitlam Scout Section! Whether your child is joining us from another group, or if it’s their first time attending Scouts, we are really excited to return back to scouting this year in a safe manner.

This announcement will contain details on how our group will be implementing Scout Canada’s recommended guidelines, as well as more information on how the first meeting will go.

Meeting Location and Time

  • Every Friday from 7:00pm to 8:30pm virtually.(See google meets details section for more information)
  • Our first meeting will be held this Friday on Jan 8th.
  • As per Scouts Canada guidelines, we will host virtual meetings under the current situation.
  • As we go later into the year, we may be able to change back to outdoor or indoor meetings depending on the situation, but all changes will be communicated with parents ahead of time.

Scouts Canada COVID 19 Prevention Measures

  • In the return to Scouting, Scouts Canada has provided guidelines for groups to follow to prevent the risk of COVID 19 spread.
  • Due to the current situation in our area, we are back to Stage 1.
  • Stage 1 states that we can only hold virtual meetings.
  • Once the situation improves, we can move onto Stage 2, where outdoor meetings can be held under strict conditions.

Google Meets Details

  • An email that contains the link to the meeting room has been sent to the parents of the Scout section.
  • You will be put into a waiting room initially, until the Scouters are ready to host the meeting. Afterwards, you will be automatically put into the room where the meeting will be held virtually.
  • There is no need to put in a password to enter the meeting.

First Meeting Details

  • This will be a parent information meeting, where Group Commissioners and Scouters will be present.
  • We will have a short introduction of each Group Commissioner and Scouter before moving onto the details on how the new group will run and how the future will look like.
  • Any questions or concerns can be answered after the session.

Outings and Camps

  • Camps have always been one of the largest components of Scouting and camping is always something that Scouts look forward to.  Unfortunately, due to the current situation, there will be no camps until the current provincial health order from Dr. Bonnie Henry has been lifted AND Scouts Canada deems our area safe enough to hold such activities again.
  • This also includes any kind of outdoor activities.

We look forward to seeing all of you this Friday, January 8th at the Google Meets virtual meeting.  If you have any questions, please send us an email.
