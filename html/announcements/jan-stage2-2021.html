Effective January 8, 2021, Scouts Canada has updated the COVID-19 guidelines to Stage 2. This means that outdoor meetings are once again permitted. You can keep yourselves updated at this link: https://www.scouts.ca/news-and-events/overview.html

In light of this new development, we will begin meeting at Scouter Pak's backyard starting this Friday, January 15 @ 7:00 - 8:30pm. The address to his house is 1280 Durant Drive in Coquitlam. We will begin working on knife skills, so bring your own pocket knives if you own one. In addition, please remember to dress for the weather, and uniforms are not mandatory.
