New Members

Anyone may apply to join Scouts Canada at any time of year. To apply to a Scouts Canada group, follow the steps that are in this article. To join our specific group, choose the 15th Coquitlam Scout Group as the group that you wish to join.

Returning Members

Memberships expire at the end of the Scouting year and must be renewed at that time. Scouts Canada usually sends an email close to the membership expiration date that provides instructions to how existing members can renew their registration. This process usually involves completing forms on myScouts. Those who do not renew their registration before the given deadline may have to apply to join the group as a new member by following the instructions in the previous section.

Help

If you need help with registration please contact either our group or the Scouts Canada Help. Visit the Contacts page for more information.
