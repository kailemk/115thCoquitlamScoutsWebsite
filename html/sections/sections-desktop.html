Overview

The 115th Coquitlam Scout Group offers a Scout section that are designed for youth with different age ranges. Both Scouting sections are focused on teaching outdoor skills and on leading youth to many exciting outdoor adventures. Some of the activities involved in these outdoor adventures may include...

  • Camping
  • Hiking
  • Canoeing
  • Kayaking
  • Snowshoeing
  • Water-sports
  • Rock-climbing
  • And much more!

Scouts

  • Ages: 11-14
  • Meeting Time: Fridays at 7:00PM to 8:30PM
  • Skills Learned: Outdoor Skills, Teamwork, Leadership, Project Planning
  • Purpose: Having fun while learning valuable leadership skills and self-confidence
