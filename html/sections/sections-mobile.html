Overview

The 115th Coquitlam Scout Group offers a Cub section and a Scout section that are designed for youth with different age ranges. Both Scouting sections are focused on teaching outdoor skills and on leading youth to many exciting outdoor adventures. Some of the activities involved at these outdoor adventures may include...

  • Camping
  • Hiking
  • Canoeing
  • Kayaking
  • Snowshoeing
  • Water-sports
  • Rock-climbing
  • And much more!

Cubs

  • Ages: 8-10
  • Meeting Time: Saturdays at 10:30AM to 12:00PM
  • Skills Learned: Outdoor Skills, Teamwork, Problem-Solving
  • Purpose: Having youth try new exciting and challenging activities

Scouts
